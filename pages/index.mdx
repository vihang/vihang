# Vihang Patel

I am a product builder. I build products for B2B, B2C and even B2B2C businesses. I have founded two companies in Enterprise software (SaaS and Big Data) and Fintech (Lending, Cards & Business Payments). One company has been sold while the other is now under my chairmanship of the Board.

I love to be creative with technology - whether it's building an MVP or designing a new feature for your site or app, nothing feels better than solving problems with tech!
